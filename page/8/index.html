知识扩展

  • 同位语从句

    • 名词 + that + 陈述句
      that引导同位语从句,应放在某些抽象名词之后,如 : idea, news, fact, problem, possibility, hope, desire, suggestion, thought…
      同位语从句对前面的名词起补充说明的作用,that在从句中不充当任何成分,只能引导同位语从句的作用,不能省略.
      Sam Benton had lost his wallet.
      The whole village soon learnt the news.
      The whole village soon learnt the news that Sam Benton had lost his wallet.

    • 名词 + whether + 陈述句
      同位语从句只能用whether引导不能用if
      My dentist asked me a question.
      “Do you like your new job?”
      My dentist asked me a question whether I liked my new job.

  • 名词 + 特殊疑问句变成陈述句语序
    When will Paul return?
    I have no idea.
    I have no idea when Paul will return.

The temoerature on earth is getting higher and higher.

改写 :
